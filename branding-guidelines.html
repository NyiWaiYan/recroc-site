Identity system

Brand guidelines

Use this sheet to keep Recroc consistent across pitch decks, social campaigns, landing pages, email headers, and branded collateral. The system is intentionally restrained: one font, a compact color set, and a clear logo hierarchy.

Typeface IBM Plex Mono
Palette Paper, Ink, Clay
Logo Recroc wordmark asset

Brand summary

Use marketing decks, posts, banners
Rule keep spacing open and deliberate
The look should feel expensive without being loud. Let the type, the logo, and the breathing room do the talking.

Type rules

  • Keep letter spacing at zero for headlines. Do not add tracking unless a label needs more air.
  • Use italics sparingly for emphasis, not as decoration.
  • Keep sentence lengths short in marketing materials so the mono face stays readable.
  • Prefer sentence case over all caps except for small metadata and section labels.

03 / Logo
Wordmark and usage
Primary mark on dark field Preferred for marketing headers
Same mark on light field Use on paper, slides, and decks

Logo rules

Clear space

Keep a margin around the logo equal to at least the height of the “R” in the wordmark.

Minimum size

Do not place the wordmark below 160 px wide on screen or below 28 mm wide in print.

Approved use

Use on clean surfaces, hero banners, social covers, pitch decks, and email signatures.

Avoid

Do not stretch, recolor, rotate, outline, or place on cluttered backgrounds that break contrast.

04 / Voice
Marketing copy cues

Write like this

Short lines. Concrete language. Calm confidence. Strong verbs. Editorial pacing.

Not like this

Too much hype, too many adjectives, or anything that sounds generic, loud, or promotional.

Best fit

Deck covers, launch graphics, case studies, Instagram captions, and campaign landing pages.
