Recroc / Homepage content brief

Content requirements for the white-theme website

Simple working brief for the content team. Keep everything clean, bright, and easy to scan. Prioritize strong visuals, short copy, and links that are ready to publish.

Audience
Content team
Scope
Homepage only
Priority
Hero video, services, recent works, about copy
01 / Required Inputs

What the team needs to deliver

  • Hero video link or file. Landscape only, ideally 16:9, with a clean frame that works on a white page.
  • At least 3 recent works with title, link, asset type, and thumbnail direction.
  • Final service list with short descriptions.
  • One approved about paragraph for the homepage.
02 / Hero Section

Use a landscape video as the main visual

The hero should feel cinematic but bright. Use a video that reads well inside a white theme and still feels premium on desktop and mobile.

  • Format: landscape video, 16:9 preferred.
  • Length: 10 to 30 seconds is ideal. Loop is fine.
  • Source: YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, or a local MP4 file.
  • Visual tone: bright exposure, strong subject, no heavy text burned into the frame.
  • Fallback: if no video is ready, use a strong still image for now.
03 / What We Do

Service list and short copy

Keep the copy short, outcome-driven, and easy to read in a quick scan. This section should answer what Recroc does in one glance.

  • Brand films and storytelling - cinematic pieces that explain the brand with clarity and emotion.
  • Photography - clean visual assets for web, social, press, and campaigns.
  • Social cutdowns - short edits made for reels, stories, and paid content.
  • Creative direction - planning, shot guidance, and visual structure before production starts.

Thumbnail rule

05 / About Text

One clean paragraph for the homepage

Recroc is a visual storytelling studio built for brands that want content with clarity, polish, and momentum. We create photo and video work that feels modern, easy to understand, and ready to use across websites, campaigns, and social channels.

06 / Delivery Checklist

Simple final pass before publishing

  • Hero media is landscape and looks good on a white theme.
  • All 3 recent works have title, link, type, and thumbnail direction.
  • Service copy is short and polished.
  • About paragraph is approved.
  • All links work and every asset is ready for upload.
